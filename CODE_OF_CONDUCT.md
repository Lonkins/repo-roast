# Code of Conduct

repo-roast is a project about jokes with a hard rule: **punch up at the code, never down at the person.** The same rule applies to this community.

## Our standard

- Be kind. Critique code, findings, and ideas — never people.
- No harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or public shaming of any individual or their repositories.
- Do not use repo-roast, its issues, or its discussions to mock a specific person's code. People roast their own repos, or opt in.
- Assume good faith; disagree with evidence.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in edited/removed content, warnings, or bans from the project spaces, at the maintainers' discretion.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainer: **tomprice13@pm.me**.
